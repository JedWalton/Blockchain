Sum of divisors

For a given range from a to b both inclusive, count sum of numbers that are divisible by n or m. Use streams to solve the problem.

For example, for range from 10 to 20 and n = 2, m = 3, there are 6 numbers that are divisible by n or m: 10, 12, 14, 15, 16, 18, 20. The sum of these numbers equals 105.


Sample Input:
10 20 2 3

Sample Output:
105



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 5 seconds
